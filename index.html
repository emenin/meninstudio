Our process

How we work

Our work solutions are as important as how we work. We want to know your story, understand who your ideal customer is and discover what makes your project unique.

Phase 1

Discover

We start with a brief questionnaire to understand your business needs and ideal customers. From there, we will build and share a strategy with you to then kick off the project.

Phase 2

Create

Time to translate your business strategy visually! Once we have a good understanding of your goals, we will start to explore and create custom made solutions.

Phase 3

Refine

Here, we invite you to be part of the process and share your valuable feedback. We will review and refine it – together – to reach the version that best fits your needs.

Phase 4

Grow

Once you are happy with the outcome, we will hand over all necessary files in relevant formats, and you will be ready to share them with the world and grow your business!
